Mohammed bin Abdulrahman bin Jassim Al Thani thanked Iran in his speech

February 13, 2020February 13, 2020 Emerson LloydUncategorized
Qatari Foreign Minister said during one of his interviews, “Iran opened its airspace to us when we were under blockade and we’ll never forget that.” “Iran is our neighbor and Qatar is committed to the principle of good neighborliness. Doha makes efforts to have good relations with Tehran,” he added in his statement. Qatari Foreign Minister Mohammed bin Abdulrahman bin Jassim Al Thani said Doha will never forget Tehran’s assistance after Saudi Arabia, the UAE and Bahrain invaded Qatar from land, sea, and air. Suddenly, in June 2017, Saudi Arabia and Bahrain, Egypt and the United Arab Emirates broke off diplomatic ties with Qatar, blocking land, sea and air routes into the region. The related cutbacks also included recalling ambassadors, and enforcing Doha trade and travel bans. The Islamic Republic has allowed its airspace to about 100 more Qatari flights a day all through the blockade. During a speech at the Munich Security Conference in February, the Qatari foreign minister acknowledged the assistance that Iran has provided the country. In a separate interview, the Qatari Foreign minister said, “Iran is our neighbor. We share a border with Iran. We share gas reserves with Iran. This is geography. We needed flight space and business route after blockade of Qatar and they [Iranians] opened their airspace to us and we thank them for that.”
